TWO WEEK FILL-IN • ONE-COLOR APPLICATION

Sanitize both your hands and your client’s hands by washing with anti-bacterial soap and misting with Refreshing Mint Anti-Spray ™. Misting hands with Anti-Spray™ prior to beginning any service enhances sanitation and helps prevent the spread of illness. Anti-Spray™ also contains thymol, which helps prevent growth of mold and fungus beneath enhancements.

Perform a Mini Manicure

1) Begin by pushing back the cuticle with a Nouveau Nail Professional Grade Combo Pusher . 2) Using a Nouveau Nail Premium Washable 100/180 File , thin the nail by about 50%. File along the cuticle area, making sure the margin of product is fush with the natural nail. 3) If needed, apply Line Eliminator ™ across the margin and blend with fle. 4) Remove dust with a clean manicure scrub brush.

5) Mist nails with Anti-Spray™ to dehydrate and cleanse surface. Wipe dry with an EZ Gauze Wipe ™.

Optional : Apply Extra Strength Primer to natural nail only, avoiding all contact with cuticle area.

Two Week Fill-In Procedure

1) Pour a small amount of Advanced Retention Nail Liquid ™ into a dappen dish. Immerse your selected Master Collection Kolinsky Acrylic Brush in the liquid. Wipe one side of your brush on the side of the dappen dish to reduce the amount of liquid absorbed into the bristles. 2) Drag your brush across the surface of the Advanced Formula Acrylic Powder ™, forming a medium-sized ball. If your mixing ratio is successful, the powder will be completely absorbed within a few seconds and the ball will appear shiny and smooth.

3) Place this ball in Zone 3, pressing from side to side and lightly extending through Zones 2 and 1. 4) Wipe your brush clean on an EZ Gauze Wipe™ between applications of acrylic. 5) If added reinforcement is needed in the stress area, add a small ball in Zone 2.

6) Allow the mixture to level on it’s own for a few seconds before pressing the product from side to side and lightly extending over the free edge. Leave a product-free margin around the side walls and cuticle area.

7) Use a Nouveau Nail Washable 100/180 File to smooth and perfect nails in a few simple steps: a) File straight down the side walls. b) File the free edges to desired shape.

c) File along the cuticle area making sure the margin of product is fush with the natural nail. d) Follow the curve of the nail, fling up one side and down the other.

Avoid fling on top of the nail. This will “fatten” the arch that was created in Zone 2.

8) Remove dust with a clean scrub brush.

9) Apply Xtreme Nail ™ Cuticle Oil to nails and massage into cuticles.

10) Buff nails with a Premium White Block Buffer to remove any remaining imperfections. 11) Wash hands thoroughly to remove all traces of oil and dust.

Finishing Options

• Polish with a Nouveau Spa Lacquer ™ and seal with a coat of Super Fast Top Coat ™.

• Apply a coat of Glass Seal ™ for a durable, glass-like fnish, followed by a coat of Super Fast Top Coat ™ for added UV protection.

• Apply a coat of Super Seal ™ UV Gel Sealer over the nails to achieve a high gloss shine, extreme durability and long-lasting protection. Avoid cuticle area and extend over the free edge to allow for shrinkage. Cure for 3 minutes.
