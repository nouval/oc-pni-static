NOUVEAU NAIL ADVANCED FORMULA WRAP SYSTEM

Our wrap system offers the fexibility of either an ultra-pure thin resin with a spray-on activator or an extra-thick brush-on wrap gel with a unique, silky smooth brush-on activator. When combined with

Sheer Strength Fabric ™, either method will result in extremely strong and long-lasting enhancements.

Spray on or brush on — the choice is yours!

SPRAY-ON APPLICATION

1) 10-Step Quick Prep (See Page 5).

2) Apply a thin layer of Advanced Formula Resin ™ to the surface of each nail. Cover entire nail up to the free edge without contacting side walls and cuticle area.

3) Mist with Super Fast Set Activator Spray ™, holding the sprayer 6" from the nails. 4) Place a strip of Sheer Strength Fabric™ on the nail plate.

5) Use scissors to trim fabric to within 1/16" of the side walls and cuticle area, leaving the free edge completely covered.

6) Ensure fabric is pressed fat to the nail.

7) Spread Advanced Formula Resin™ over each nail surface, making sure to completely saturate the fabric without contacting side walls and cuticle area.

8) Mist with Super Fast Set Activator Spray™, holding the sprayer 6" from the nails. 9) For added strength, repeat Steps 7 and 8.

10) Once nails have set, use a 4-way buffer to smooth any imperfections. 11) Remove dust with a clean scrub brush.

12) Apply Xtreme Nail ™ Cuticle Oil to nails and massage into cuticles. 13) Buff to a smooth fnish.

14) Wash hands thoroughly to remove oil and dust.

Finishing Options

• Polish with a Nouveau Spa Lacquer ™ and seal with a coat of Super Fast Top Coat ™.

• Apply a coat of Glass Seal ™ for a durable, glass-like fnish, followed by a coat of Super Fast Top Coat ™ for added UV protection.

• Apply a coat of Super Seal ™ UV Gel Sealer over the nails to achieve a high gloss shine, extreme durability and long-lasting protection. Avoid cuticle area and extend over free edge to allow for shrinkage. Cure for 3 minutes.
