LIQUID & POWDER SYSTEMS

FOUR WEEK french FILL-IN • TWO-COLOR APPLICATION

Sanitize both your hands and your client’s hands by washing with anti-bacterial soap and misting with Refreshing Mint Anti-Spray ™. Misting hands with Anti-Spray™ prior to beginning any service enhances sanitation and helps prevent the spread of illness. Anti-Spray™ also contains thymol, which helps prevent growth of mold and fungus beneath enhancements.

Perform a Mini Manicure

1) Begin by pushing back the cuticle with a Nouveau Nail Professional Grade Combo Pusher . 2) Using a Nouveau Nail Washable 100/180 File , thin the nail by about 50%. File along the cuticle area making sure the margin of product is fush with the natural nail. 3) If needed, apply Line Eliminator ™ across the margin and blend with fle.

Four Week French Fill-In Procedure

1) Use your PNI25 High Power Electric File ™ with your choice of either a Golden Carbide ™ bit or a Real Diamond ™ bit. Following above the smile line, ditch out a valley between the free edge and Zone 2 for a “new set” look every time.

2) Pour a small amount of Advanced Retention Nail Liquid ™ into a dappen dish. Immerse your selected Master Kolinsky Collection Acrylic Brush in the liquid. Wipe one side of your brush on the side of the dappen dish to reduce the amount of liquid absorbed into the bristles. 3) Drag your brush across the surface of the White Advanced Formula Acrylic Powder ™, forming a medium-sized ball. If your mixing ratio is successful, the powder will be completely absorbed within a few seconds and the ball will appear shiny and smooth.

4) Apply the white ball to Zone 1 to create your smile line. Lay bead in the middle of the free edge and, using the body of your brush, guide the product up to one corner and then to the other, creating the depth of your smile. Using the tip of the brush, defne and shape the smile. 5) Wipe your brush clean on an EZ Gauze Wipe ™ between applications of acrylic. 6) Create your second ball with the powder color of your choice and place in Zone 2. 7) Allow the mixture to level on it’s own for a few seconds before pressing the product from

side to side and lightly extending over the free edge. Zone 2 is extremely important. This is the thickest part of the nail and reinforces the stress area.

8) Your fnal ball will be placed in Zone 3. This smaller ball of product will be pressed from side to side and lightly extended through Zones 2 and 1. Leave a product-free margin around the sid walls and cuticle area.

9) Follow Steps 9-13 for French Acrylic Application (See Page 8).

Finishing Options

• Apply a coat of Glass Seal ™ for a durable, glass-like fnish, followed by a coat of Super Fast Top Coat ™ for added UV protection.

• Apply a coat of Super Seal ™ UV Gel Sealer over the nails to achieve a high gloss shine, extreme durability and long-lasting protection. Avoid cuticle area and extend over the free edge to allow for shrinkage. Cure for 3 minutes.
