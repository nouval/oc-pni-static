NOUVEAU NAIL ADVANCED FORMULA WRAP SYSTEM

BRUSH-ON APPLICATION

1) 10-Step Quick Prep (See Page 5).

2) Apply a coat of Brush-On Wrap Gel to the entire nail, avoiding side walls and cuticle area. 3) Brush on Smooth Set ™ Brush-On Activator to set gel.

4) In order to preserve the condition of the brush, alternate caps between Smooth Set™ Brush-On Activator and Brush Kleen ™ frequently.

5) Place a strip of Sheer Strength Fabric ™ on the nail plate.

6) Use scissors to trim fabric to within 1/16" of the side walls and cuticle area, leaving the free edge completely covered.

7) Ensure fabric is pressed fat to the nail.

8) Apply another coat of Brush-On Wrap Gel, making sure to completely saturate the fabric. 9) Brush on Smooth Set™ Brush-On Activator to set the gel. 10) For added strength, repeat Steps 8 and 9.

11) Once nails have set, use a 4-way buffer to smooth any imperfections. 12) Remove dust with a clean scrub brush.

13) Apply Xtreme Nail ™ Cuticle Oil to nails and massage into cuticles. 14) Buff to a smooth fnish.

15) Wash hands thoroughly to remove oil and dust.

Finishing Options

• Polish with a Nouveau Spa Lacquer ™ and seal with a coat of Super Fast Top Coat ™.

• Apply a coat of Glass Seal ™ for a durable, glass-like fnish, followed by a coat of Super Fast Top Coat ™ for added UV protection.

• Apply a coat of Super Seal ™ UV Gel Sealer over the nails to achieve a high gloss shine, extreme durability and long-lasting protection. Avoid cuticle area and extend over free edge to allow for shrinkage. Cure for 3 minutes.
