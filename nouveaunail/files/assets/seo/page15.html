FRENCH UV GEL APPLICATION

PINK AND WHITE UV GEL APPLICATION

1) 10-Step Quick Prep (See Page 5).

2) Saturate an EZ Gauze Wipe ™ with Finishing Wipe for quick clean up of the brush, as needed. 3) Lift a small amount of White One-Step UV Gel with your gel brush.

4) Beginning in the center of the free edge, create the desired depth of your smile line, extending the gel just over the edge to allow for shrinkage after curing. Clean your brush as needed. 5) Before curing, perfect your smile line by retracing with a clean gel brush. 6) Complete all four fngers on the frst hand.

7) Lay the nails in front of a Nouveau Nail Mini Tunnel UV Lamp for 10 seconds before entering to prevent burning sensation.

8) Set nails under lamp for 2 minutes. 9) Repeat Steps 3-8 on second hand. 10) Repeat Steps 3-8 on both thumbs.

11) Drag brush through Pink One-Step UV Gel , as you would through acrylic powder, keeping the gel to one side of the brush.

12) Beginning at the center of the nail, work gel back toward the cuticle area and up to, but not covering, the free edge. Do not brush, but guide, the gel by “skating” the surface.

13) Use the brush to gently cover zones 2 & 3, leaving a margin around the sidewalls and cuticle area. 14) Complete all 4 fngers on the frst hand.

15) Dip a birchwood stick in Finishing Wipe to gently clean around the sidewalls and cuticle area. Any gel left in these area will cause lifting.

16) Lay the nails in front of Nouveau Nail Mini Tunnel UV Lamp for 10 seconds before entering to prevent burning sensation.

17) Cure nails under lamp for 2-3 minutes. 18) Repeat Steps 11-17 on second hand. 19) Repeat Steps 11-17 on both thumbs

20) Drag brush through Crystal Clear One-Step UV Gel , as you would through acrylic powder, keeping the gel to one side of the brush.

21) Beginning at the center of the nail, work the gel back toward the cuticle area, down to and over the free edge, extending to allow for shrinkage after curing. Do not brush, but guide, the gel by “skating” the surface.

22) Complete all four fngers on the frst hand.

23) Dip a birchwood stick in Finishing Wipe to gently clean around the side walls and cuticle area. Any gel left in these areas will cause lifting.

24) Lay the nails in front of Nouveau Nail Mini Tunnel UV Lamp for 10 seconds before entering to prevent burning sensation.

25) Cure nails under lamp for 2-3 minutes. 26) Repeat Steps 20-25 on second hand. 27) Repeat Steps 20-25 on both thumbs.

28) After the fnal curing, saturate an EZ Gauze Wipe™ with Finishing Wipe and completely remove the tacky layer.

29) Use a Nouveau Nail Premium Washable Nail File to break any seals needed in the side walls. 30) If needed, buff any imperfections with an Premium White Block Buffer . 31) Remove dust with a clean scrub brush.

32) Apply a coat of Super Seal ™ UV Gel Sealer to each nail, avoiding the

cuticle area and extending over the free edge. Cure for 3 minutes. 13
