ADVANCED rETENTION ACryLIC SySTEM

NOUVEAU NAIL ADVANCED rETENTION ACryLIC SySTEM is the most advanced formula liquid and powder system available today. The Advanced Retention Nail Liquid is formulated with UVA and UVB inhibitors to help prevent yellowing. The Advanced Formula Acrylic Powders are a unique formula that combines the adhesion properties of a hard polymer with the fexibility of a soft polymer. Together the liquid and powder create beautiful acrylic nails that boast truly superior adhesion and durability.

SINGLE COLOr APPLICATION

1) 10-Step Quick Prep (See Page 5).

2) Pour a small amount of Advanced Retention Retention Liquid ™ into a dappen dish. Immerse your selected Master Collection Kolinsky Acrylic Brush into the liquid. Wipe one side of your brush on the side of the dappen dish to reduce the amount of liquid absorbed into the bristles. 3) Drag your brush across the surface of the Advanced Formula Acrylic Powder , forming a medium-sized ball. If your mixing ratio is successful, the powder will be completely absorbed within a few seconds and the ball will appear shiny and smooth.

4) Apply clear ball to Zone 1 to create a “mock” smile line. It is not necessary to take as much time creating the smile line of a one color application as it is with a two color application. 5) Wipe your brush clean on an EZ Gauze Wipe ™ between applications of acrylic.

6) Create your second ball of clear acrylic and place in Zone 2. Allow the mixture to level on it’s own for a few seconds before pressing the product from side to side and lightly extending over the free edge. Zone 2 is extremely important. This is the thickest part of the nail and reinforces the stress area. 7) Your fnal ball will be placed in Zone 3. This smaller ball of product will be pressed from side to side and lightly extended through Zones 2 and 1. Leave a product-free margin around the side walls and cuticle area.

8) If this is a sculpted nail, please use the process for C-Curve Sticks ™ Shaping Tools .

9) Use a Nouveau Nail Washable 100/180 File to smooth and perfect nails in a few simple steps: a) File straight down the side walls. b) File the free edges to desired shape.

c) File along the cuticle area making sure the margin of product is fush with the natural nail. d) Follow the curve of the nail, fling up one side and down the other.

Avoid fling on top of the nail. This will “fatten” the arch that was created in Zone 2.

10) Remove dust with a clean scrub brush.

11) Apply Xtreme Nail ™ Cuticle Oil to nails and massage into cuticles.

12) Buff nails with a Premium White Block Buffer to remove any remaining imperfections. 13) Wash hands thoroughly to remove all traces of oil and dust.

Finishing Options

• Polish with a Nouveau Spa Lacquer ™ and seal with a coat of Super Fast Top Coat ™.

• Apply a coat of Glass Seal ™ for a durable, glass-like fnish, followed by a coat of Super Fast Top Coat ™ for added UV protection.

• Apply a coat of Super Seal ™ UV Gel Sealer over the nails to achieve a high gloss shine, extreme durability and long-lasting protection. Avoid cuticle area and extend over the free edge to allow for shrinkage. Cure for 3 minutes.
