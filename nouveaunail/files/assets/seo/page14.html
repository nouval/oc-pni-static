NOUVEAU NAIL ONE-STEP UV GEL SYSTEM

Our best-selling single process gel system boasts easy application, an ultra-glossy fnish and incredible strength and durability.

ONE-STEP “ORIGINAL FORMULA” UV GEL APPLICATION • ONE COLOR

1) 10-Step Quick Prep (See Page 5).

2) Saturate an EZ Gauze Wipe ™ with Finishing Wipe for quick clean up of brush, as needed. 3) Drag brush through One-Step UV Gel as you would through acrylic powder, keeping the gel to one side of the brush.

4) Beginning at the center of the nail, work gel back toward the cuticle area and forward toward the free edge. Do not brush, but guide, the gel by “skating” the surface.

5) Use the brush to gently cover the entire nail, leaving a margin around the side walls and cuticle area. 6) Extend the gel just over the free edge to allow for shrinkage after curing. 7) Complete all four fngers on the frst hand.

8) Dip a birchwood stick in Finishing Wipe to gently clean around the sidewalls and cuticle area. Any gel left in these area will cause lifting.

9) Lay the nails in front of a Nouveau Nail Mini Tunnel UV Lamp for 10 seconds before entering to prevent burning sensation. 10) Cure nails under UV lamp for 2 to 3 minutes. 11) Repeat Steps 3-10 on second hand. 12) Repeat Steps 3-10 on both thumbs.

13) Add a second layer of gel to both hands by repeating Steps 3-10 without removing tacky layer between curings.

14) After the fnal curing, saturate an EZ Gauze Wipe™ with Finishing Wipe and completely remove the tacky layer.

15) Use a Nouveau Nail Premium Washable File to break any seals needed in the side walls. 16) If needed, buff away any imperfections with a Premium White Block Buffer . 17) Remove dust with a clean scrub brush.

18) Apply a coat of Super Seal ™ UV Gel Sealer to each nail, avoiding the cuticle area and extending over the free edge. Cure for 3 minutes.

No tacky layer to remove! Extra glossy fnish with added durability!
