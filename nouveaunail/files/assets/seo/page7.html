The first ten steps toward success with all

Nouveau Nail enhancement systems

Step 1 • Sanitize both your hands and your client’s hands by washing with anti-bacterial soap and misting with Refreshing Mint Anti-Spray ™. Misting hands with Anti-Spray™ prior to beginning any service enhances sanitation and helps prevent the spread of illness.

Step 2 • Perform a mini manicure. Begin by pushing back the cuticle area with a Nouveau Nail Professional Grade Combination Pusher .

Step 3 • Remove shine from natural nail using a Nouveau Nail Premium Washable 100/180 File .

Step 4 • If sculpting, apply forms and skip to Step #9.

Step 5 • If using tips, select appropriate style and sizes of Nouveau Nail Professional Nail Tips and adhere to natural nails using Extra Strength Nail Glue .

Step 6 • Cut tips to desired length with a tip slicer and shape with fle.

Step 7 • Apply Line Eliminator ™ across the tip seam and blend with fle.

Step 8 • Remove shine from nail tips with fle.

Step 9 • Remove dust with a clean manicure scrub brush.

Step 10 • Mist nails with Refreshing Mint Anti-Spray™ to dehydrate and cleanse the nail surface. Wipe dry with an EZ Gauze Wipe ™. Anti-Spray™ also contains thymol, which helps prevent growth of mold and fungus beneath enhancements.

Optional Step • Apply Extra Strength Primer to natural nail only, avoiding all contact with cuticle area. Extra Strength Primer is available in both an acid formula and an acid-free formula. This step is highly recommended for clients prone to lifting.
